Here are a few of my favorite toys! I'm very discerning, and love playtime.

1. My absolute favorite toy is my wand toy! The little toys on the end change sometimes. Once they get worn out, Mom attaches a new one. I've had two bees and a fish so far! I love chasing this toy around the house, and will drag it everywhere with me.

2. The first toy I ever owned is my second favorite: a pipe cleaner with a little pink feather attached. They sent me home with that one from the shelter, and I still love it.

3. My third-favorite toy is my sparkle ball! It's a litte pink pompom with pink sparkles on it. I love to chase it, especially under doors so my mom has to go pick them up.

4. Last but not least, I love my little plastic springs! I love the way they bounce, click, and clatter on the floor. I chase those under doors and furniture too :)

Thanks for reading about me :3
