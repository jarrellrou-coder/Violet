Hi, there! My name is Violet, and I'm a black domestic shorthair living in Maryland. I was born in Brooklyn, New York in May of 2023. Yes, I'm a gemini.

My mom adopted me on August 13th of 2023, when I was about three months old. My name was "Catmint" then, and I was still living at the ACC Shelter (go Boroughbreds!) with my sisters Rosemary and Thyme.

I was asleep when my mom first came into the shelter, and she looked at allll the other adult cats there who needed a home, but none of them were able or wanted to go home with her.

When I woke up, there she was! I knew she was my mom, even if she didn't. I marched over to the front of the cage to yell at her and let her know she was meant to be my mom. Hey, lady! It's me!

As soon as she held me, I snuggled into her neck. No way was I letting her go now that I'd found her! She took me home in a big cardboard carrier a few minutes later. We've been best friends ever since!

Thanks for reading about me :3
